What is CiviConnect?

CiviConnect is a diverse team of passionate individuals dedicated to creating innovative solutions for underserved communities. Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable, and sustainable internet connectivity to rural and remote areas. By harnessing renewable energy and decentralized networking, we aim to bridge the digital divide, ensuring that no community is left disconnected, especially in times of crisis. Our ultimate goal is to empower these communities by enabling access to essential services, education, and opportunities through dependable communication infrastructure.

What is the solution?

CiviConnect is a solar-powered, self-healing mesh Wi-Fi network designed to provide reliable and affordable internet connectivity to underserved communities, including refugee camps and rural areas.
